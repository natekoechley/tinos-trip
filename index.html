🌬️ The wind guide

The meltemi blows from the north / northeast. Simple rule: south & southwest coasts stay calm; the north coast gets hammered. It's a windy week, so favour the sheltered list.
🛡️ Most protected — go here
Kionia (SW, near Hora) — flat + sheltered Agios Sostis / Agios Romanos / Agios Ioannis Porto (south bays) Kardiani Bay (Ormos Giannaki / O Ntinos) — named protected Small Kolymbithra cove — calm even in a strong northerly Hora waterfront & Ktikados (villa)
〰️ Partial / breezy but fine
Isternia Bay — west-facing cove Pyrgos square — gusty, fine for a seated coffee
🌬️ Exposed — expect strong wind
Big Kolymbithra & Panormos (north coast — surf beaches) Sunset bar above Kardiani — high & west-facing; have a backup Volax plateau & hilltop monastery — bring a layer Remote NE beach — brutal, skip
Good news on the booked dinner: O Ntinos sits on Kardiani Bay, which is specifically sheltered from the north wind — a safe pick for Tuesday evening. The one swap to remember: for any beach, skip big Kolymbithra (north coast, blown out); use the small Kolymbithra cove or Kionia.
