🇬🇷 Tinos — Four Nights

Cyclades family trip · base in Ktikados
Sun Jun 21 → Thu Jun 25, 2026 · two rental cars · party of 6
The whole trip in one idea: the two cars are Nonnie's mobility system. At every stair-maze village, one car drops her at a flat anchor and stays; the other parks so the roamers can explore. One gentle "active" stop per day for her, with Wednesday as a rest day.

🌬️ The wind guide

The meltemi blows from the north / northeast. Simple rule: south & southwest coasts stay calm; the north coast gets hammered. It's a windy week, so favour the sheltered list.
🛡️ Most protected — go here
Kionia (SW, near Hora) — flat + sheltered Agios Sostis / Agios Romanos / Agios Ioannis Porto (south bays) Kardiani Bay (Ormos Giannaki) — named protected Small Kolymbithra cove — calm even in a strong northerly Hora waterfront & Ktikados (villa + dinners)
〰️ Partial / breezy but fine
Isternia Bay — west-facing cove Pyrgos square — gusty, fine for a seated coffee
🌬️ Exposed — expect strong wind
Big Kolymbithra & Panormos (north coast — surf beaches) Sunset bar above Kardiani — high & west-facing; have a backup Volax plateau & hilltop monastery — bring a layer Remote NE beach — brutal, skip
The one swap it forces: don't count on big Kolymbithra for Tuesday's beach. Use the small Kolymbithra cove, or move the beach moment to Wednesday at Kionia — sheltered, flat, and reliable all week.
